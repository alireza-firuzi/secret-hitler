Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Secret Hitler Online ("the Application") collects, uses, and discloses information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

Our main goal is to provide a fun, privacy-respecting online multiplayer experience. We do not require any registration, email addresses, or social media logins to use the Application.

  • Gameplay Display Names: When you join or create a game lobby, you choose a temporary nickname. This name is shared with other players in your game lobby and is not linked to your real identity.
  • Game Data: We temporarily transmit game lobby codes, list of active players in the lobby, and game board state (votes, roles, policies enacted) to synchronize gameplay in real-time.
  • Device Identifiers: The Application generates a random local identifier (e.g. user_xxxxxx) to distinguish you from other players in the active game lobby. This ID is stored in memory and is not used for tracking or advertising.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Establish and maintain your connection to the game lobby.
  • Synchronize the state of the game board in real-time between all participants.

3. Data Retention

All active game states and lobby data are stored temporarily in transient memory on our WebSocket sync servers. Game records are not archived or permanently stored after the game ends or becomes inactive.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not share, sell, or disclose your information to any third parties. We do not integrate any advertising SDKs, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics services.

5. Security

We prioritize the security of your connection. All communication between the Application and the multiplayer servers is encrypted using Secure WebSockets (wss://).

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or feedback regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via our GitHub repository: github.com/alireza-firuzi/secret-hitler.
